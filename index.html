ABOUT ME

My creative research lies in experimentation with recyclable materials, particularly plastics, and applying them to the context of wearable objects.

Plastics must be given a second chance of being useful. My intention is to explore their plasticity as material and their aesthetic possibilities as a valuable resource, making a comparison with fine metals which are highly valued, both financially and culturally.

I want to bring focus to the relationship of fine metals and plastics, as they have an extremely long life span (somewhat perpetual), can be made beautiful, and also can be bound together again, repurposed.
